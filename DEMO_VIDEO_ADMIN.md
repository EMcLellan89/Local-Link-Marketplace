"Territory Partner System & Admin Dashboard - Local Link Marketplace Management"

Explore the powerful backend of Local Link Marketplace: territory-based partner program, comprehensive admin controls, deal approval workflows, and platform-wide analytics. Perfect for potential partners and system administrators.

## INTRODUCTION (0:00 - 2:00)

### Hook

**Script:**
"What if you could own exclusive rights to a geographic territory and earn recurring commissions on every business deal, loyalty program, and service sold in that area? Today I'm revealing the territory partner system that powers Local Link Marketplace's nationwide expansion."

### What to Show
- Map visualization of territories across a region
- Commission calculator showing potential earnings
- Partner dashboard with real revenue numbers

### Platform Overview

**Script:**
"Local Link operates on a three-tier model:
1. **Customers** use the platform for free
2. **Merchants** pay monthly subscriptions ($79-$159)
3. **Territory Partners** earn commissions on everything merchants generate

This creates a scalable business model where everyone wins. Let me show you both sides - the partner experience and the admin controls that make it all work."

## TERRITORY PARTNER PROGRAM EXPLAINED (2:00 - 5:00)

### Section 1: Partner Business Model (2:00 - 3:00)

**What to Show:**
Display infographic showing:
- Territory structure
- Commission breakdown
- Revenue streams
- Growth potential

**Script:**
"Territory partners are independent business owners who:
- Get exclusive rights to a defined geographic area
- Sign up local businesses to the platform
- Earn ongoing commissions on:
  - Monthly merchant subscriptions (20%)
  - Loyalty program fees (25%)
  - Add-on service purchases (15%)
  - Deal revenue (5%)
- Can expand into adjacent territories based on performance

This isn't a one-time commission - partners earn recurring revenue as long as the merchant remains active."

### Section 2: Revenue Example (3:00 - 4:00)

**What to Show:**
Animated calculator:

**Example Territory: Population 50,000**
- Sign up 20 merchants
- Average tier: $99.99/month
- 15 merchants add loyalty: $49/month
- Average monthly deal revenue: $5,000 per merchant

**Partner Monthly Income:**
- Subscription commissions: $399.96 (20% of $1,999.80)
- Loyalty commissions: $183.75 (25% of $735)
- Deal commissions: $5,000 (5% of $100,000)
- Add-on services: Variable (~$500/month average)

**Total Monthly Recurring: $6,083.71**

**Script:**
"Let's look at realistic numbers. A territory partner with 20 active merchants earning around $6,000 per month in recurring commissions. As merchants succeed and add more services, partner income grows. Plus expansion opportunities into adjacent territories."

### Section 3: Partner Requirements (4:00 - 5:00)

**What to Show:**
Checklist graphic:

**Script:**
"Partner requirements are straightforward:
- Business experience or sales background
- Commitment to sign minimum 10 merchants in first 90 days
- Active engagement with territory merchants
- Professional representation of the brand
- Quarterly business reviews with admin team

No franchise fees. No ongoing royalties beyond the agreed commission split. You're building an asset that generates recurring income."
