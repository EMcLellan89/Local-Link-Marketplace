**What to Show:**

2. Show active deals list
3. Click on the deal you want to redeem
4. QR code displayed full screen
5. Business scans code (demonstrate if possible)
6. Confirmation of redemption
7. Deal moves to "Redeemed" section

**Script:**
"Redeeming is the easiest part. Go to your purchases page, find the deal, and show the QR code to the business. They scan it, and you're done. The deal is automatically marked as redeemed so you can't accidentally use it twice."

- Check expiration dates
- Know the business hours
- Read any special instructions
