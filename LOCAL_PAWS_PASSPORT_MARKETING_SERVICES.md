## Commission Structure for Partners (Tier-Based)

Your revenue share on marketing services depends on your Partner subscription tier:

### Partner Tier ($69/month) & Master Partner ($179/month):
- **80% revenue share** on all marketing services
- **One-Time Services:** 80% of project fee
- **Referral Bonuses:** $200 for merchant services referral

### White-Label Partner ($549/month):
- **70% revenue share** on all marketing services
- **One-Time Services:** 70% of project fee
- **Referral Bonuses:** $200 for merchant services referral

### Example Partner Earnings (Partner Tier at 80%):

**Scenario 1: 10 Merchants with Basic Services**
- 10 merchants × $697/month (Starter Package) = $6,970/month
- Your 80% share = **$5,576/month recurring**

**Scenario 2: Mixed Service Portfolio**
- 5 Starter Packages ($697) = $3,485
- 3 Growth Packages ($1,497) = $4,491
- 2 Premium Packages ($2,997) = $5,994
- Total MRR: $13,970
- Your 80% share = **$11,176/month recurring**

**Scenario 3: Enterprise Territory**
- 20 merchants with average package of $1,200/month
- Total MRR: $24,000
- Your 80% share = **$19,200/month recurring**
- Plus one-time services and bonuses = $20,000-25,000/month potential

**Note:** White-Label Partners earn 70% but have custom branding, API access, and can scale to 100 territories.
