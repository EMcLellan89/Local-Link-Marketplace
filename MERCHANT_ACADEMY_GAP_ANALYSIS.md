# Merchant Academy — Gap Analysis & Opportunities

## 🎯 WHAT WE DISCOVERED

Partners have **23 DFY services** they sell to merchants.
Partners have **26 playbooks** teaching them HOW TO SELL those services.
Merchants have **26 courses** teaching them to run their business.

**The Gap:** Many services partners SELL have NO corresponding "DIY" merchant course.

---

## 💡 THE OPPORTUNITY

Create merchant courses teaching them to DO IT THEMSELVES for services partners currently fulfill.

**Why this works:**
- Merchants who can't afford partners can DIY
- Merchants who hire partners understand the value better
- Creates upsell path: Try DIY → Hire partner to do it
- More educated customers = better results
