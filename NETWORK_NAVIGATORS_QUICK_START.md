## Partner Sales Process

### For Partners Using This Page:
1. **Discovery:** Partner qualifies merchant using Facebook Monetization playbook
2. **Share Link:** Partner shares referral link: `/merchant/network-navigators?ref={partner-slug}`
3. **Merchant Explores:** Merchant reviews options on dedicated page
4. **Purchase:** Merchant selects territory and/or DFY services
5. **Attribution:** System attributes sale to partner (permanent)
6. **Commission:** Partner earns 20-25% on all purchases
7. **Fulfillment:** Partner can claim DFY jobs for extra income
