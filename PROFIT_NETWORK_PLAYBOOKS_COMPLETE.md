## Each Playbook Includes

### 1. Business Description
Full explanation of what the business is and how it works.

### 2. Target Audience
Detailed breakdown of:
- Who needs this product
- Industry verticals
- Business size/revenue
- Pain points they're experiencing

### 3. Selling Strategy
Step-by-step approach:
- Opening hooks
- Discovery questions
- Demonstration tactics
- ROI calculations
- Closing techniques

### 4. Facebook Advertising Info
- Ad targeting strategies
- Creative concepts
- Lead magnets
- Retargeting sequences
- How your tracking link is embedded

### 5. Commission Information
- Exact commission per sale
- Recurring vs one-time
- Average customer lifetime value
- Payment frequency

### 6. Year-One Projections
Three scenarios for each business:

**Conservative**
- Minimal effort, safe estimates
- Part-time promotion
- Example: 2-5 sales per month

**Moderate**
- Average performance
- Consistent effort
- Example: 8-15 sales per month

**Aggressive**
- High effort, maximum results
- Full-time focus
- Example: 20-50 sales per month

### 7. Sales Scripts
Pre-written scripts for:
- Cold outreach
- Discovery calls
- Objection handling
- Closing conversations

### 8. Common Objections
Typical objections with proven responses:
- "It's too expensive"
- "We already have something"
- "Not sure it will work for us"
